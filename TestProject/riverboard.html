The Riverboard

Made from locally felled and milled Pennsylvania White Oak and Walnut, as well as food safe epoxy.

One side of the board is an end grain cutting board meant for work, and the second side is a hand carved epoxy river charcuterie board meant for serving.

Each epoxy riverbed is free-hand carved without templates or guides to make each board absolutely unique.
